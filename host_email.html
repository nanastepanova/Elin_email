Welcome to Elin.ai!

Your company cares about how your work affects you, and they made the first step in making it better. Your company installed Elin.ai as part of your Zoom account!

What is Elin.ai you ask? Elin enables distributed teams to be engaged, happy, connected and driven by the same vision. It generates smart analytics based on employees' Zoom calls. You can learn about your team’s engagement, wellbeing, communication, and overall company culture. Elin even makes suggestions for the improvements!

Why do you receive this email? Because you’re one of the Zoom hosts in your company. You’ll be the one scheduling and leading the calls, so it’s important you know what it’s all about.

How does it affect YOU? When you finish a recorded Zoom call with your team, we’ll send you a summary email that will include
  • Call transcript (finally you don’t have to take notes and can focus on the discussion!)
  • Participants list (need to send that follow-up email? We got you.)
  • How did this call affect the participants (impact on Mood, Engagement, Wellbeing, and Community feel)
Important to remember.
  • Elin only works when you record the meeting to the cloud. You have a choice of whether to record meetings or not, but we encourage you to record calls where team participates in active discussions, makes decisions together or updates. We’ll store it safely. Click here to see how to change settings to record to the cloud.

  • To receive a better quality of the transcript, we need to hear you better. Please change settings to preserve original call sound. Click here to change settings to preserve original sound.

We appreciate your feedback. A lot! When you receive your first summary email - please take a minute and click on ‘Feedback’ button to tell us what you find useful and what you would like to see.

Speak to you soon!

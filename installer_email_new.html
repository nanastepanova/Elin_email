What is Elin?

Elin enables distributed teams to be engaged, happy, connected and driven by the same vision. She generates smart analytics based on employees' interactions with our Slack bot and general Slack activity.

You will learn about your employees’ engagement, wellbeing, communication, and overall company culture. Elin will then support you in making data-driven decisions on the improvements. What’s not to like?!

How does it work?
Elin becomes your new teammate on Slack! She interacts with employees, asks a couple of simple questions every day and generates a good-looking Dashboard to reflect employees’ mood, engagement, wellbeing, communication patterns, and perceived impact.

Once a week we’ll send a summary of aggregated data to CEO and include actionable recommendations for improvement.

You can find details on our website: How Elin works.
